This is TinkerHub! A community devoted to promoting learning, making and tinkering.

Find Out More

Who are we? What do we do?

We are a community of makers and hackers, working in tandem to make change and spread knowledge! We put our own spin to things that already exist, conduct workshops for locals, kids and students, connect and share ideas.

How can I be a part of this?

Do a talk

Passionate about something? Come over and talk about it!

Conduct a workshop

Come share your skills to a group. Be it software, hardware or anything in between and beyond.

Help organise

Chip in to help out TinkerHub, plan and help conduct events, write content and more.

spread the love

Spread the word about TinkerHub, to your friends, collegues, neighbours and family.
